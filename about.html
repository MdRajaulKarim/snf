About the Project

Scientific Name Finder makes biodiversity knowledge accessible to everyone — students, researchers and curious minds alike. All data is sourced live from the world's largest open biodiversity database.

🎯

Our Mission

To promote awareness of biodiversity and make scientific knowledge accessible to everyone through a clean, responsive, and intuitive platform powered by real global data.

🌐

Data Source

All species data is sourced live from Global Biodiversity Information Facility — the world's largest open biodiversity database providing verified data, including 8 million+ species records and 1.9 billion+ occurrences.
